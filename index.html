ABOUT

Hi I'm Ryan 👋

I believe that there are a lot of problems out there that just need someone to reach out a helping hand. I apply methods of design thinking in order to reach out that hand.

I strive to empathize with people on a deep level so that I can understand their needs and design a solution.

A Bit About My Past

When I was a kid me and two of my friends discovered a deep passion for random acts of kindness. We noticed that we were always at our happiest point when we were making others happy, even if it was something small. So we started leaving little notes for people, helping them carry groceries to their car in the snow, giving people flowers. Nothing big but little things. Looking back this taught me so much about what it means to be a designer. It taught me what I call the “reverse midset”. When you completely change your mindset from what you are trying to achieve what someone else is trying to achieve.
